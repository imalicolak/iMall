About

The story behind I_MALL

  • March 30th, 2023

    Our Humble Beginnings

    With project three begginning it's course, groups seemed to fill up quickly. However, 3 misfits were left stranded in breakout rooms trying to figure out who they would team up with. Then they found eachother.

  • March 31st, 2023

    An Agency is Born

    The three quickly got over their feelings of not being selected by other groups and realized that maybe this was destiny calling. Something great was on the horizon!

  • April 3rd, 2023

    Early Challenges

    Before success could hit, the team ran into many hurdles to begin, making them question whether this was really fate. Their original idea for project three was rejected, their lead designer, Brandon had issues syncing his windows PC virtual environment with the database, and none of them were too keen on the django system. But finally fire struck, and a once and a lifetime idea came along. I_MALL was born!

  • April 7th, 2023

    I_MALL is launched!

    With the due date looming over their heads or all week, they were able to successfully complete I_MALL on time and launch it. The site attracted over 200,000 viewers and generated 6 million in revenue in its first 12 hours!
